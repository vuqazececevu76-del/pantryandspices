Privacy Policy

**1. Intro**

Your privacy is important to us, and we're serious about keeping your info safe. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you book travel with us.

**2. What Info We Grab**

*   **From You (Travelers):**
    *   Name, email, phone number, and login details
    *   Passport or ID (if the booking needs it)
    *   Trip plans like flights, hotels, and car rentals
    *   What you like for travel, and any rewards programs you're in, or special requests
*   **Payment Stuff:**
    *   Credit/debit card info and billing address
    *   What you've paid for and any cancellations
*   **Techy Stuff:**
    *   Your IP address, what kind of device and browser you're using
    *   What you've searched for, booked, and other actions on our site
    *   Cookies (little data trackers) and analytics

**3. How We Put Your Data to Use**

We use your info to:

*   Make and handle your account
*   Deal with bookings and payments
*   Send confirmations, updates, or let you know if something's canceled
*   Suggest trips you might like
*   Give you deals, discounts, or rewards (if you want them!)
*   See how the site's doing and ways to make it better
*   Stop fraud and keep things secure
*   Help you out if you have questions or issues

**4. Who We Share With**

We might share your data with:

*   **Airlines, Hotels, and Rental Car Companies:** So they can complete your bookings
*   **Payment Companies:** To handle transactions safely
*   **Analytics and Hosting Folks:** They help us keep things running smoothly
*   **The Law:** If we have to share because the law says so

We don't sell your personal info.

**5. Cookies**

We might use cookies to:

*   Keep you logged in
*   Remember your preferences and searches
*   See how the site's working
*   Recommend trips and deals

You can control your cookies in your browser settings.

**6. How Long We Keep Your Data**

We keep your booking, payment, and account info as long as your account is open or if we need it for legal or business reasons. You can ask us to delete your data.

**7. Your Rights**

You can:

*   See, change, or get rid of your personal data
*   Check out your travel and booking history
*   Stop getting marketing emails
*   Change your cookie settings

**8. Security**

We use security measures to protect your data. Make sure to keep your account info safe.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

Our site isn't for anyone under 13. We don't try to collect data from kids.

**10. Policy Updates**

We might update this policy sometimes. The newest version will be posted here with the date it was changed.
